Password Strength Checker

Password Strength Criteria

  • Weak:- Less than 5 characters or contains only letters or numbers. (e.g., "abc", "12345")
  • Moderate:- A combination of letters and numbers, approximately 8 characters. (e.g., "abc12345", "password1")
  • Strong:- A combination of letters, numbers, and symbols, length between 8-12 characters. (e.g., "Abc@1234", "Str0ng!2025")
